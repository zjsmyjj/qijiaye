# Qijia Ye

PhD Candidate | Annenberg School for Communication, University of Pennsylvania

I am a PhD candidate at the Annenberg School for Communication at the University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on health communication, persuasion, and the use of generative AI in social science research.

My work examines how generative AI can be used to generate, evaluate, and refine health messages, with particular attention to methodological innovation, message pretesting, and experimental design.

More broadly, I am interested in how emerging AI tools can support communication research while preserving rigor, validity, and ethical sensitivity.

## Research interests

- Health communication
- Persuasion
- Generative AI
- Emotions
- Moral Norms
